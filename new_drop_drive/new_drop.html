Frequently Asked Questions
  • How do I become a driver?
  • Click on ‘Start Earning’ or ‘Become a driver’ above to sign up. You can also sign up as a driver via visiting our website www.dropoff.ng and clicking on “become a driver.” Alternatively, you can sign up through the app after downloading the app from Google Playstore (for android devices) and Apple store (for iOS devices). On the website or app, select your member type (individual in this instance), fill in the required details in the provided registration form and click on submit. You will receive an SMS and e-mail from us to confirm your account registration. Having done this, you will need to provide certain documents for our review while visiting our office for an onboarding session which includes a thorough background check. You must also be at least 21 years old at the time of registering your account. As soon as this is done, you can go online to receive ride requests.
  • How often do i get paid?
  • To be eligible for driving on our platform, you would need to provide the following documents for our review: - Valid driver’s license - Vehicle registration and allied documents - Vehicle Insurance certificate - Proof of residential location
  • How do I get paid by riders?
  • As a driver, you get paid by the rider through cash or debit card. When payment is made via card, do not collect cash from the rider. If payment is to be by card, this is displayed in the app as soon as the trip commences.
  • How much can I make?
  • You can make any amount you want depending on how long you can stay online and how many trips you can make. Averagely, you can make 100,000 in a week!
  • What is your commission?
  • 10% only.
  • How do I go online?
  • To go online and start accepting ride requests, simply login to your app using your registration details and tap on “go online”.
